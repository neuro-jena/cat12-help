Links, sources, and recommendations

  • IXI database
    Dataset of anatomical images with different modalities of over 550 healthy subjects.

  • BWP (Brain Web Phantom)
    Most important validation dataset for methods that rely on T1/T2 or PD-weighted data(Collins 1998).

  • Hammers atlas
    Anatomical atlas of 20 healthy subjects that is used within this toolbox.

  • Anatomy toolbox
    SPM toolbox with cytoarchitectonic atlas maps.

  • MS (Multiple Sclerosis) toolbox
    Estimation of MS lesions in T1+T2 images.
